# QR Payments

Universal QR Payments enables the application to support processing of multiple QR formats. including Bharat QR, UPI Qr and PHQR. QR Payments related to transactions like P2P(Person to person), P2M(Person to Merchant) and P2B(Person to Biller) can be configured.

Specifications related to the QR format is configured and validations with respect to length, expected value, required for the transaction type can be performed through configuration.

Payments for QR transactions are supported through open banking API standards.  &#x20;
